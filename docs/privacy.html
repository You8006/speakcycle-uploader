1. Introduction

Welcome to SpeakCycle ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service, which automates the posting of English learning videos to TikTok.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 TikTok Account Information

When you authorize our application with TikTok, we may collect:

  • Basic Profile Information: Your TikTok display name, avatar, and open ID (via user.info.basic scope)
  • Access Tokens: OAuth tokens to post content on your behalf

2.2 Usage Information

  • Video posting history and schedules
  • Service interaction logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Post educational English learning videos to your TikTok account
  • Maintain and improve our service
  • Communicate with you about service updates
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns

4. TikTok API Usage

Our application uses the following TikTok API features:

  • Login Kit: To authenticate your TikTok account
  • Content Posting API: To upload and publish educational videos

We only request the minimum necessary permissions (scopes) to provide our service:

  • user.info.basic - To identify your account
  • video.publish - To post videos to your profile
  • video.upload - To upload video content

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Access tokens are stored securely and encrypted
  • We do not store your TikTok password
  • Data is transmitted using secure HTTPS connections

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only:

  • With TikTok, as required to provide our service
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Revoke: Disconnect our app from your TikTok account at any time
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data from our systems

To revoke access, you can disconnect SpeakCycle from your TikTok account settings or contact us directly.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our service. When you disconnect our app:

  • Access tokens are deleted immediately
  • Account information is removed within 30 days

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@speakcycle.site
